Instructions

1. First Keep your laptop screen straight

2. Move yourself 3-4 feet away from screen

3. As you move your right wrist in an ascending and a descending manner, a red dot should appear on the right wrist.

4. Press the play button, and move yourself 3-4 feet away again

5. The red paddle is the paddle, that will move according to your right wrist.
